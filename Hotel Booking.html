Taj Usha Kiran Palace

Nestled in Gwalior, adjacent to Jai Vilas Palace, the Taj Usha Kiran Palace is a heritage hotel steeped in history. Built in 1902, this former royal guest house turned opulent retreat boasts 50 rooms and suites within nine acres of landscaped gardens. It showcases intricate architecture, offering a glimpse into the regal past of the Scindia dynasty with modern luxury.
